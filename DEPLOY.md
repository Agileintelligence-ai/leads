# Agile Energy Solar Lead Qualifier — Deployment Guide

## Deploy to Railway (Free, 5 mins, no credit card)

### Step 1 — Create a GitHub account (if you don't have one)
Go to github.com and sign up free.

### Step 2 — Upload the code to GitHub
1. Go to github.com/new
2. Name it: agile-solar-qualifier
3. Click "Create repository"
4. Click "uploading an existing file"
5. Drag BOTH files into the box: server.js and index.html
6. Also drag package.json
7. Click "Commit changes"

### Step 3 — Deploy on Railway
1. Go to railway.app
2. Click "Start a New Project"
3. Click "Deploy from GitHub repo"
4. Connect your GitHub account
5. Select "agile-solar-qualifier"
6. Railway auto-detects Node.js and deploys it
7. Click your deployment → Settings → Generate Domain
8. You get a URL like: https://agile-solar-qualifier.up.railway.app

### Step 4 — Share with your team
Send the URL to anyone on your team.
They enter their own API keys in the app (saved in their browser).

## That's it!
The app is now live at a real URL your whole team can access.
Railway free tier = 500 hours/month which is plenty.
